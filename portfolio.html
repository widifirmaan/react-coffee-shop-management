2026 PRODUCT SHOWCASE

REACT - COFFEE SHOP
MANAGEMENT BRUTALIST

A BOLD, HIGH-PERFORMANCE OPERATIONS SYSTEM FOR MODERN CAFFEINE CRAFTSMEN.

01

01. THE FRONT DOOR

The Desktop Landing Page features a high-impact greeting with dynamic parallax elements. It's designed to captivate customers the moment they arrive, combining aesthetic flair with essential information.

02

02. CONTROL CENTER

The Management Dashboard provides at-a-glance metrics for sales, inventory status, and team communication via a sticky-note system. It's the central nervous system for shop operations.

03

03. THE STORY

An immersive "Info Layer" that slides over the main interface to tell the brand's story. This module focuses on connection and philosophy, moving beyond simple transactions.

04

04. SELF-SERVICE KIOSK

The digital ordering interface allows customers to browse the menu, customize their drinks, and input table details directly. A friction-less path from craving to coffee.

05

05. CONTENT ENGINE

A full-featured Blog CMS that handles news, events, and promotions. The list view provides high visibility for all published and draft content with categorized filtering.

06

06. READABLE DESIGN

The Blog Post Reader prioritizes typography and whitespace, ensuring that shop news is delivered in a beautiful, distraction-free environment.

07

07. BOLD FEEDBACK

System alerts are designed with Neo-Brutalist intensity to ensure critical information is never missed. High-contrast colors and thick borders drive immediate user attention.

08

08. INSTANT SERVICE

The "Call Waiter" module bridges the gap between digital and physical service. One tap notifies the staff exactly where assistance is needed, streamlining the customer experience.

09

09. DATA AT A GLANCE

Visualizing sales trends and revenue through integrated charting modals. The design keeps data interpretation simple and fast for busy managers.

10

10. SECURE CHECKOUT

The payment interface handles multiple methods while maintaining the signature brutalist look. It provides clear transaction summaries before final processing.

11

11. SAFETY FIRST

Confirmation modals provide an extra layer of security for destructive actions, using clear, unambiguous messaging to prevent accidental data loss.

12

12. STAFF DATA

A detailed staff editor that manages roles, access levels, and personal profiles. The interface remains clean despite the complexity of the data fields.

13

13. VOICE OF CUSTOMER

The feedback interface collects qualitative data from patrons, allowing the management to continuously improve service quality based on real reviews.

14

14. ACCESSIBLE INPUT

Integrating feedback triggers directly into the footer ensures that the bridge between customers and management is always just one click away on any page.

15

15. STOCK CONTROL

The inventory management page tracks ingredient levels and units. Automated thresholds alert staff when supplies are running low, preventing stockouts.

16

16. KITCHEN COMMAND

A high-visibility Kitchen Display System (KDS) that manages order flow in real-time. Orders move from PENDING to SERVED with a clear, color-coded status hierarchy.

17

17. GATEKEEPER

The login interface provides secure access for different roles (Manager vs Staff). It sets the tone with a clean, centered layout and bold interactive elements.

18

18. VISUAL MENU

A grid-based menu catalogue optimized for visual appeal. Large imagery and clear pricing labels make browsing easy for both customers and staff.

19

19. PRODUCT CRUD

Managing the product line-up is effortless with a dedicated CMS. Managers can update prices, availability, and categories with real-time sync across the platform.

20

20. AUDIT TRAIL

Comprehensive logging of all transactions. Staff can revisit past orders to handle disputes or verify details, ensuring transparency in every sale.

21

21. WORKFORCE SCHEDULER

A drag-and-drop interface for managing weekly staff shifts. It includes built-in role validation to ensure every shift has the necessary staff types (Barista, Manager, etc.).

22

22. GLOBAL CONFIG

Control the shop's identity from one place. Update social links, visual themes, and technical configurations without touching a single line of code.

23

23. TEAM DIRECTORY

Centralized database for all employees. It manages active status, contact info, and internal credentials to keep the team organized and secure.

24

24. FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE

Deep dive into daily, weekly, and monthly revenue. The interface provides the clarity needed to make data-driven decisions for business growth.

25

25. SERVICE QUEUE

A specialized view for service staff to track customer requests. It ensures no "Waiter Call" goes unanswered, improving overall floor efficiency.

26

26. POCKET-SIZED POWER

The mobile version of the landing page adapts the neo-brutalist aesthetic for smaller screens, maintaining visual impact and usability on the go.

27

27. RESPONSIVE STORY

Even complex overlays like the "Info Layer" are optimized for mobile touch, ensuring the brand narrative is accessible to every user, everywhere.
